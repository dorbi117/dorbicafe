So, you've stumbled across my page! This is a little project I've wanted to tackle for ages.

I've always wanted to open my own cafe. While that isn't the most practical thing at the moment, I figured it's definitely possible to open a virtual cafe. I've wanted to foster a cozy, comfortable space to talk about books, games, tvv shows & movies, as well as nerdier stuff like homelabbing and self hosting.

So, this is that space. Grab a cup of coffee or tea, and browse at your leisure through the pages. My goal is to take back at least one sliver of the internet, and make it feel like the internet of yesteryear. I long for the days when the internet was not so deeply siloed, when people were the ones in control. It's not much, but hopefully someone finds this page and takes a moment to pause, take a deep breath, and remember what the old internet was like.
