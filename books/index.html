Looking for a good book? I have a few recommendations!

This is a place where I'll be posting about books, whether they're the books I'm currently reading, or the books that have made a lasting impact on me. Feel free to sit down with a cup of coffee from the cafe. This one is on the house.

All Time Favorites

  • Yumi & the Nightmare Painter, by Brandon Sanderson

    Yumi is a beautiful novel, and was a breath of fresh air from my favorite author, Brandon Sanderson.

  • The Hobbit, by J.R.R. Tolkien

    The Hobbit is a book that I re-read annually. For some reason, it has become a holiday book for me; something I return to when the weather gets cold, and I find myself wanting to sit down with an old friend, and a cup of hot cocoa.

Currently Reading

  • Dungeon Crawler Carl [series], by Matt Dinniman

    Currently, I'm on book 3 of the DCC series. It's been really fun, and has served as almost a palette cleanser of sorts. I can sit down with Carl & Donut, turn off my brain, and enjoy the shenanigans they always get up to.
